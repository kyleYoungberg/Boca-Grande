Backstory:

Leonard Herrera growing up was always a very talented illustrator and often received many complements on his work and his good childhood friend Andrew Jordan even told him many times that he should make movie posters when he grew up. Now graduated top of his class at Princeton Leonard had gone farther than just movie posters with his work being featured in magazines, promotional materials for many corporations around the globe, and even being the lead director in graphic design for the hit game Time Busters. Now with all of his experience in the field he has now founded Boca Grande Graphics to continue in his passion for design.
